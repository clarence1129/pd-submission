About Me

I am Clarence Phua, a passionate UI/UX and creative designer based in Singapore. From a young age, I have always been fascinated by how design can shape human experiences, turning complex interactions into something intuitive, beautiful, and meaningful.

My journey into the world of digital design began with curiosity — exploring how interfaces, visuals, and storytelling combine to create seamless experiences for users. Over the years, I have honed my skills across a variety of platforms, from web applications to interactive prototypes, and even immersive VR environments.

I thrive on solving design challenges, thinking critically about user needs, and creating work that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Every project I take on is an opportunity to learn, experiment, and push the boundaries of creativity.

My Design Philosophy

Emotion & Connection

I am inspired by culture, color, and interaction. I create designs that not only meet users' goals but also evoke emotion, spark curiosity, and leave a lasting impression.

Collaboration & Growth

Communication is central to my process. I enjoy working with teams to translate ideas into tangible outcomes while continuously learning from feedback and perspectives.

Innovation & Purpose

My goal is to craft digital experiences that feel effortless, intuitive, and meaningful. I approach each challenge with creativity, dedication, and a desire to make a difference.
