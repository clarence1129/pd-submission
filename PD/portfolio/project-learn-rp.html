Learn@RP

A comprehensive academic management platform for tracking learning progress

Project Overview

Learn@RP is a mobile application designed to help Republic Polytechnic students effectively manage their academic journey. The app provides a centralized platform for tracking learning progress, monitoring assignment deadlines, and staying informed about upcoming exam dates.

This project focuses on delivering an intuitive and visually appealing user interface that empowers students to take control of their academic commitments and track their performance in real-time. With comprehensive UI/UX design, the app seamlessly integrates all essential academic information into one convenient platform.

Key Features

  • Learning Progress Tracking - Visualize your academic progress across subjects and modules
  • Assignment Management - View all upcoming assignments with deadlines and submission status
  • Exam Schedule - Stay updated with exam dates, times, and locations
  • Progress Analytics - View detailed performance metrics and progress reports
  • Smart Notifications - Receive timely reminders for deadlines and important dates
  • User-Friendly Dashboard - Clean, intuitive interface for quick access to all information

Design Methodology

UI/UX Design Figma User Research Wireframing Prototyping Mobile Design Design Systems

My Role

I led the UI/UX design for this academic management application. My responsibilities included creating comprehensive design systems, developing intuitive user interfaces, and designing interactive prototypes. I focused on user research to understand student pain points and created wireframes and high-fidelity mockups that prioritize usability and visual consistency across all screens. The design emphasizes clarity, accessibility, and efficiency to help students quickly find the information they need.

Design System & UI Kit

The UI Kit serves as the foundation for the entire Learn@RP application. It contains a comprehensive collection of reusable components, design tokens, and visual guidelines that ensure consistency across all screens. This system includes buttons, cards, input fields, navigation patterns, typography scales, and color systems. The design system was meticulously crafted to support scalability, maintainability, and future expansion while maintaining a cohesive visual language throughout the application.
